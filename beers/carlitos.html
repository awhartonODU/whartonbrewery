Instructions

  1. Add 8.000 lb Munich Malt, 5.000 lb Pilsner (2 Row) UK, 4.000 oz Special B Malt, 4.000 oz Caramel/Crystal Malt - 60L, 4.000 oz Chocolate Malt (UK), 4.000 oz Caramel/Crystal Malt - 120L to the mash tun.
  2. Bring 4.900 gal water to 173.517 F, 8.000 gal water to 187.747 F for upcoming infusions.
  3. Add 4.900 gal water at 173.517 F to mash to bring it to 155.000 F. Hold for 60.000 min.
  4. Add 8.000 gal water at 187.747 F to mash to bring it to 165.200 F. Hold for 15.000 min.
  5. Bring the wort to a boil and hold for 60.000 min.
  6. Put 2.000 oz Fuggles into boil for 60.000 min.
  7. Put 0.000 tsp Whirlfloc into boil for 15.000 min.
  8. Stop boiling the wort.
  9. You should have 6.500 gal wort post-boil. You anticipate losing 1.000 gal to trub and chiller loss. The final volume in the primary is 5.500 gal.
  10. Cool wort and pitch WLP002 - English Ale Yeast Ale yeast, to the primary.
  11. Let ferment until FG is 1.019.
  12. Transfer beer to secondary.
