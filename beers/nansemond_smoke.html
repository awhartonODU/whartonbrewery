Instructions

  1. Add 2.200 lb Munich Malt - 10L, 12.000 oz Briess - Aromatic Malt, 4.000 oz Acid Malt, 12.000 oz Honey Malt, 8.000 oz Caramunich Malt, 7.150 lb Briess (cherry wood) smoked malt to the mash tun.
  2. Bring 4.060 gal water to 140.764 F, 1.863 gal water to 212.000 F, 4.887 gal water to 194.101 F for upcoming infusions.
  3. Add 4.060 gal water at 140.764 F to mash to bring it to 132.000 F. Hold for 30.000 min.
  4. Add 1.863 gal water at 212.000 F to mash to bring it to 155.000 F. Hold for 30.000 min.
  5. Add 4.887 gal water at 194.101 F to mash to bring it to 165.200 F. Hold for 15.000 min.
  6. Bring the wort to a boil and hold for 60.000 min.
  7. Put 0.750 oz Mount Hood into boil for 60.000 min.
  8. Put 1.000 oz Hallertau into boil for 20.000 min.
  9. Put 0.000 tsp Whirlfloc into boil for 15.000 min.
  10. Put 0.500 oz Strisselspalt into boil for 1.000 min.
  11. Put 0.500 oz Mount Hood into boil for 1.000 min.
  12. Stop boiling the wort.
  13. You should have 5.500 gal wort post-boil. You anticipate losing 0.000 tsp to trub and chiller loss. The final volume in the primary is 5.500 gal.
  14. Cool wort and pitch WLP862 - Cry Havoc Lager yeast, to the primary.
  15. Let ferment until FG is 1.016.
  16. Transfer beer to secondary.
  17. Put 0.250 oz Strisselspalt into fermenter for 60.000 min.
  18. Put 0.250 oz Crystal into fermenter for 60.000 min.
