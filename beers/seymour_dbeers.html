Instructions

  1. Add 4.000 lb Pale Malt (2 Row) US, 2.500 lb Weyermann - Pale Wheat Malt, 2.500 lb Vienna Malt to the mash tun.
  2. Bring 6.025 gal water to 168.051 F, 3.720 gal water to 182.032 F for upcoming infusions.
  3. Add 6.025 gal water at 168.051 F to mash to bring it to 156.000 F. Hold for 60.000 min.
  4. Add 3.720 gal water at 182.032 F to mash to bring it to 165.200 F. Hold for 15.000 min.
  5. Bring the wort to a boil and hold for 60.000 min.
  6. Put 0.500 oz Citra into boil for 60.000 min.
  7. Put 0.000 tsp Whirlfloc into boil for 15.000 min.
  8. Put 0.500 oz Citra into boil for 5.000 min.
  9. Stop boiling the wort.
  10. You should have 5.345 gal wort post-boil. You anticipate losing 0.000 tsp to trub and chiller loss. The final volume in the primary is 5.345 gal.
  11. Cool wort and pitch WLP008 - East Coast Ale Yeast Ale yeast, to the primary.
  12. Let ferment until FG is 1.012.
  13. Transfer beer to secondary.
