Instructions

  1. Add 7.000 lb Pale Malt (2 Row) US, 1.000 lb Oats, Flaked, 1.000 lb Roasted Barley, 12.000 oz Caramel/Crystal Malt - 60L, 12.000 oz Chocolate Malt (US), 10.000 oz Munich Malt, 8.000 oz Barley, Flaked to the mash tun.
  2. Bring 4.069 gal water to 171.462 F, 8.300 gal water to 182.255 F for upcoming infusions.
  3. Add 4.069 gal water at 171.462 F to mash to bring it to 159.000 F. Hold for 90.000 min.
  4. Add 8.300 gal water at 182.255 F to mash to bring it to 165.200 F. Hold for 15.000 min.
  5. Bring the wort to a boil and hold for 90.000 min.
  6. Put 0.300 oz Magnum into boil for 60.000 min.
  7. Put 0.000 tsp Whirlfloc into boil for 15.000 min.
  8. Put 1.000 oz Kent Goldings into boil for 10.000 min.
  9. Stop boiling the wort.
  10. You should have 6.425 gal wort post-boil. You anticipate losing 1.000 gal to trub and chiller loss. The final volume in the primary is 5.425 gal.
  11. Cool wort and pitch WLP001 - California Ale Yeast Ale yeast, to the primary.
  12. Let ferment until FG is 1.011.
  13. Transfer beer to secondary.
  14. Put 4.000 oz Chocolate Extract into secondary for 0.000 s.
  15. Put 4.000 oz Peanut Butter Extract into secondary for 0.000 s.
